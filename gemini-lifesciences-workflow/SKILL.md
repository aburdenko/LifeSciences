# Gemini Life Sciences Workflow

This skill provides procedural knowledge and domain expertise on how Gemini Enterprise accelerates the lifecycle of a molecule in the life sciences industry.

## Overview

The lifecycle is divided into eight key stages:
1. **Discovery**: Using Co-Scientist Agent and OneMCP for experimental design.
2. **Research and Preclinical**: Leveraging AlphaFold 2, AlphaGenome, and TxGemma for simulations and ADMET profiling.
3. **Clinical Design**: Enhancing trial design with stratification and dose escalation modeling.
4. **Clinical Operations**: Driving "Zero-Query Trials" via semantic QC and multimodal reconciliation.
5. **Regulatory Operations**: Automating document drafting (CMC, CSR) using NotebookLM.
6. **Regulatory Intelligence**: Preempting health authority queries and automating responses.
7. **Medical Engagement**: Providing personalized, guarded medical query responses.
8. **Commercial Reach**: Generating compliant promotional media with Creative Media Studio.
